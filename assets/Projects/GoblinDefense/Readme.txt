INSTRUCTIONS:

Click on a tower in order to instance it, click somewhere
else in order to place it(if you have enough money)

Press Right-click on a tower to sell it.

Sound On/off on the upper-right corner.

Upgrades are in the upgrades menu:
			Damage upgrade
			Attack speed upgrade
			Tower cost upgrade
Press the speed button in the down-right corner in order to speed up the game.

Press esc in the menu window in order to close the game.
